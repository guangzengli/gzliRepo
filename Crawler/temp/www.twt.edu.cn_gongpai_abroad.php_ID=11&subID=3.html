The Best Memories in France

Another unforgettable experience was that I gave a presentation to all the people working with liquid calculation excel document—a calculation tool we use for simple network calculation. Most of them were not clear enough about the excel document, which was established by an engineer who has left. So on presentation, I showed how to use it according to my understanding. Although there were still some confusions at the very beginning, we discussed, explained, exchanged opinions, did an example, and at last every one was clear about it. I didn’t think my work is so important to them before, and then I felt proud to do this, really from my bottom of heart. So with the manuals, one can understand how do these calculation tools work, how to use these calculation tools, and what must be taken into account when do calculation.


……


Every morning, we greeted each other by kissing face or shaking hands. In China people never kiss face except for lovers and couples, but in France, family, friends, colleagues, even the first-met people send greetings like this. After greeting, I always have a good mood to start my work and the mood can last a whole day. I accepted the way of greeting and began to like it. It’s a pity that we Chinese don’t do like this.
